DigitalSoul

DigitalSoul is a gamified, culturally sensitive, spirituality-integrated, mental health app designed for research and clinical use in acute inpatient settings at Harvard's Mclean Hospital.

Searching for DigitalSoul…

How do we make all patients feel truly heard in acute psychiatric settings across the globe? Can recent advances in digital technology enable us to provide culturally sensitive transdiagnostic care in a way that is both engaging and intuitive for acute psychiatric patients? Can the act of creating a digital mental health app be made more inclusive if it is built by the interfaith multi-national team? Our team at Harvard’s Mclean Hospital sought this out by creating DigitalSoul.

DigitalSoul Framework & Features


DigitalSoul is based on Dr. David’s H Rosmarin’s groundbreaking transdiagnostic SPIRIT group therapy program which has been used for years in Harvard’s Mclean Hospital to provide patients a safe space to explore how their spirituality may intersect with their mental health. DigitalSoul takes the core principles of SPIRIT and translates them into a gamified self-directed digital platform that leverages cutting-edge behavioral economics to motivate patients. Our hope is DigitalSoul will eventually provide low-cost options to provide more personalized care.

Animated Reflection

We wanted this app to have cohesive visual language and narrative flow to improve engagement. As such, we developed a unique visual style design to be respectful of people across the globe and optimized it as an affordable way to create custom animation for this project.

Blooming Questions

Are multiple-choice questions ever fun? But for acute patients dealing with cognitive issues, trying to answer standard survey-style questions cognitively can be very difficult. We devised a way to make it a bit easier and hopefully a little less bland.

Cross Platform Compatibility

DigitalSoul is optimized for cross-device compatibility on iOS and Android devices. You can start a lesson on your iPhone and finish it on your Android tablet.

See Your Progress

It can be frustrating for patients when their progress is unclear. To help with this, DigitalSoul provides gamified encouragement, colorful progress indicators, and easy ways to track improvement

Meet The DigitalSoul Team

Research

Dr. David H Rosmarin

Creator of DigitalSoul

Contact: drosmarin@mclean.harvard.edu

Dr. Rosmarin is the PI on the DigtalSoul project which translate his groundbreaking SPIRIT protocol into a self-directed DigitalPlatform. Dr. Rosmarin is the director of the Spirituality and Mental Health Program at McLean Hospital and an associate professor of psychology in the Department of Psychiatry at Harvard Medical School. Dr. Rosmarin's program of research examines the relevance of spiritual/religious issues to psychopathology and its treatment. He has published over 100 manuscripts, editorials, and chapters, and served as co-editor of the Handbook of Spirituality, Religion and Mental Health.
